HELLO!

Welcome to my IBM Work Experience Review

From the 3rd April 2017 - 7th April 2017 I was able to secure a work experience with the wonderful company IBM. Over the week I talked to many people in different parts of IBM, different projects and was even introduced to an Intern! Everyone was very accommodating and was able to give me tons of useful information, advice and terms that I can use later on in life. Before coming to IBM I had never heard of design thinking, scrum methodology, or agile delivery. Even softwares and tools such as: Zeplin, Brackets, Atom, Node Red, Handlebars, SCSS, IBM Watson, Bluemix, KPI's and numerous others. All these tools are used by many IBM employees on a daily basis and help them carry out their necessary work and tasks. For information and security reasons specific projects cannot be mentioned however they were very interesting and insightful.
