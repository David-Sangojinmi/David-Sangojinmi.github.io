My Projects

Name: Matrix. Software: Processing. Description: Based on the original movie I was messing around with a different program I made that lined up iterations of balls and bounced them from the top of the screen to the bottom and back. I changed the colour to green RGB(0, 255, 0), and adjusted the speeds as well as the opacity each time it clears the screen. It produced a similar, but not exact effect of when the numbers roll down the screen in the matrix.

Name: Mulit-directional rain. Software Processing. Description: Rain effect which can be toggled to come from all four directions. 'w' and 'e' for toggling top-down rain. 'a' and 's' for toggling left-right rain and 'd' and 'f' for toggling right-left rain.
